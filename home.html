About the event

  • You are required to make a video review of an automobile or a component or a technology for a maximum time period of five minutes
  • Your video will be uploaded in IGTV.
  • Based on the Instagram response (50%) and critical evaluation by judges (50%), 10 best videos will be selected
  • Selected videos will be posted in our YouTube channel for critical review by viewers. Videos with plagiarism and wrong information will be discarded if allegations are proven.
  • Scores for like and share in the YouTube channel will added to the above score to select the top 3 positions

No age limits

  • If you are an automotive enthusiast and have a flair in making a review video of an automobile, component or a technology to impress audience, this is the platform for you.
  • No age limits
  • Individual and team entries accepted

Rules and Regulations

  1. All entries must be a digital video of full HD resolution (1920 X 1080) preferably in horizontal orientation.
  2. Each entry must focus on any one topic related to AUTOMOBILE: For example
    1. Review of a car/bike of your choice
    2. Review the working of a component
    3. Review of a technology
  3. Participants can submit the entries groups or as individuals.
  4. d. The length of the video should not exceed five minutes, including the 10 second title screen (see clause ‘e’) in the beginning and 5 second cast and crew list at the end.

Evaluation Criteria

  1. Initial Round
    • 50 Marks will be given to the Instagram likes and share with equal weightage. Video with maximum like and share will be given 50 Marks.
    • Other videos will be graded relatively. Auto-likes and shares will result in your direct termination from the competition
    • 30 Marks will be given for the automotive technical content of the review. False information can result in negative marking
    • 30 Marks will be given for the automotive technical content of the review. False information can result in negative marking
    • 20 Marks for your skills in videography, editing and sound clarity

  2. Final Round
    • Based on the evaluation, top 10 teams qualify for final round
    • These 10 videos will be uploaded in our YouTube channel in which viewers can critically review the work. People can point out flaws in the video, plagiarism or copy right issues if any. If found guilty, your video will be removed from TOP 10 list.
    • Also, you will be asked to submit the references for your video upon selection into TOP 10 list
    • People can like and share your video in YouTube channel and a maximum bonus score of 20 marks will be added to previous grading to select the winners
